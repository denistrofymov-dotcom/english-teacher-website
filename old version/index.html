About Me

I graduated from Karazin Kharkiv National University with a Master's degree in English and Literature. In the early 2000s, I studied and taught in London at Birkbeck, University of London.

In 2010, I made aliyah to Israel, and in 2012 successfully completed teacher training at Haifa Teacher's Training College, receiving my teaching license.

I've been teaching in high school since 2012, specializing in Bagrut preparation for 3-5 units. I create an English-speaking environment using current resources: YouTube, media, blogger videos, Quizlet, and dynamic presentations.
